Subscription & Refund Policy

Subscriptions renew monthly or annually until canceled. You may cancel through the available customer portal; cancellation normally takes effect at the end of the current paid billing period.

Except where required by law, subscription payments are generally non-refundable after a billing period begins. Contact support promptly if you believe there was a duplicate charge, technical billing error, or unauthorized transaction.

Upgrades, downgrades, credits, and prorations are handled according to the terms displayed in Stripe Checkout or the customer portal.

Service problems
